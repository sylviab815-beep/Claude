## Alternative Approaches

### Name Generation Tips

* Use names from different cultural backgrounds
* Consider historical names from specific time periods
* Use occupation-based surnames (Baker, Fisher, Carpenter) sparingly
* Mix common first names with unusual surnames and vice versa
* Research actual demographic data for your setting's time/place

### Place Name Alternatives

* Research actual small town names in your region
* Consider geographic features (river bends, hill formations)
* Use indigenous place names appropriately
* Consider historical naming patterns
* Blend or modify real place names

### Business Name Alternatives

* Research actual local business naming patterns
* Consider family names of founders
* Use local geographic references
* Research historical business names
* Consider cultural/ethnic influences in naming
