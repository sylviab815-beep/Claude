# KDP Info Document Template — Romantic Comedy

Create a companion .docx file with everything needed to publish on Amazon KDP.

## Required Sections

### Book Title & Subtitle
Subtitle should signal tone and tropes clearly — e.g., "A Sweet Small-Town Romantic Comedy" or "A Clean Workplace Rom-Com."

### Author
From the outline or as specified by the user.

### Book Description
Max 4000 characters. Structure for rom-com:

1. **Hook line** — one funny or intriguing sentence that immediately signals rom-com energy
2. **H1 intro** — who she is, her flaw, what she wants (and what she thinks she wants)
3. **H2 intro** — who he is, why they clash
4. **The comedic collision** — what forces them together and why that's a disaster
5. **The stakes** — what's at risk emotionally AND comedically
6. **Closing hook** — tease the comedy and the chemistry, make them want to click Buy

Use HTML-compatible formatting. Include keywords naturally. The description should make people smile before they finish reading it.

### Amazon Keywords (7 phrases)
Mix broad and specific:
- 2 broad rom-com terms: "clean romantic comedy", "sweet contemporary romance"
- 2 trope-specific: "enemies to lovers romantic comedy", "fake dating clean romance"
- 1 diversity/audience signal if applicable: "multicultural romance", "diverse romantic comedy"
- 1 setting/situation: "workplace romantic comedy", "small town clean romance"
- 1 mood: "funny romance books", "laugh out loud romance"

### BISAC Categories (3 suggestions)
Primary categories for clean rom-com:
- FICTION / Romance / Romantic Comedy (primary — always include)
- FICTION / Romance / Contemporary (usually secondary)
- FICTION / Christian / Romance (if faith elements present)
- FICTION / Women (if strong women's fiction crossover)
- FICTION / Humor / General (if comedy is a major selling point)

### Suggested Price
- Ebook: $2.99-$4.99 (novellas lower, novels higher)
- Paperback: $12.99-$15.99
- Note: Clean/sweet rom-com is a KU-dominant market (80-90% of top performers are KU enrolled) — strongly recommend KU enrollment

### Series Information
- Series name suggestion
- Book number
- Standalone or continuing characters?
- Notes on recurring cast/setting for future books

### Comparable Titles
3-5 real published clean/sweet rom-com titles. Include:
- Title and author
- Why it's comparable
- Publication date (last 3 years preferred)

### Target Audience
- Age range (clean rom-com skews broad: 25-65+)
- Reading habits (KU readers, book club, faith-based readers?)
- What emotional experience they're seeking
- Authors they likely enjoy

### Cover Image Prompt

**IMPORTANT**: Current market data confirms illustrated/cartoon covers are the standard signal for romantic comedy in the clean/sweet market. Stock photography covers are associated with older or higher-heat romance. Specify an illustrated style.

A detailed prompt for AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Ideogram):

1. **The exact book title** as text on the cover
2. **A placeholder for the author name** at the bottom
3. **Visual style**: Illustrated/cartoon style. Bright, warm color palette. Playful and modern. This is the market standard for clean rom-com and signals "sweet and contemporary" to browsers.
4. **Character or scene description** specific to THIS book — the leads, a key comedic moment, the setting
5. **Diversity note**: If the leads are diverse/multicultural, include this in the cover prompt — market data shows diverse covers perform well in this category
6. **Composition**:
   - Title in top third
   - Author name at bottom
   - Portrait orientation (1600x2560px)
   - Bright, high-contrast palette that thumbnails well

## Formatting
US Letter, 1 inch margins, Heading 1 for sections, 12pt Times New Roman, single spacing.
