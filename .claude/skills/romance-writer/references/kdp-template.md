# KDP Info Document Template

Create a companion .docx file for the manuscript with the following sections. This document provides everything needed to publish on Amazon KDP.

## Required Sections

### Book Title
The full title of the manuscript.

### Subtitle
A subtitle suggestion that hints at tone and tropes (e.g., "A Rivals-to-Lovers Romantic Comedy" or "A Steamy Small-Town Romance").

### Author
Use the author name from the outline or as specified by the user.

### Book Description
Write a compelling Amazon book description, max 4000 characters. Structure:
1. **Hook line** — one gripping sentence
2. **Character A intro** — who they are, what they want
3. **Character B intro** — who they are, what they want
4. **The collision** — what forces them together
5. **The stakes** — what's at risk (emotional, not just plot)
6. **Closing hook** — a question or tease that makes readers click "Buy"

Use HTML-compatible formatting (bold for emphasis) since Amazon supports it. Include relevant keywords naturally.

### Amazon Keywords (7 phrases)
Seven keyword phrases optimized for Amazon search. Mix broad and specific:
- 2-3 broad genre terms (e.g., "romantic comedy books", "steamy paranormal romance")
- 2-3 trope-specific terms (e.g., "enemies to lovers romcom", "forced proximity dark romance")
- 1-2 audience-specific terms (e.g., "LGBTQ+ romance", "spicy romance books")

### BISAC Categories (3 suggestions)
Three BISAC category codes with full names. Always include the most specific category available.

### Suggested Price
- Ebook: $2.99-$4.99 (adjust based on length — novellas on the lower end, novels higher)
- Paperback: $12.99-$16.99
- Note on KDP Select / Kindle Unlimited eligibility

### Series Information
- Series name suggestion (if from an outline that includes series potential)
- Book number in series
- Whether it stands alone
- Brief notes on potential future books

### Comparable Titles
3-5 real, published books in similar genres. Include:
- Title and author
- Why it's comparable (trope overlap, tone, audience)
- Publication date (ideally within last 3 years)

### Target Audience
Describe the ideal reader:
- Age range
- Reading preferences
- What they're looking for in this book
- Which other authors they likely enjoy

### Cover Image Prompt

A detailed, ready-to-use prompt for AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Ideogram, etc.) that describes the ideal cover.

**The prompt must include:**

1. **The exact book title** rendered as text on the cover
2. **A placeholder for the author name** at the bottom
3. **Visual style** matching current market trends for the subgenre:
   - Romcom: Illustrated/cartoon style, bright pastel palette
   - Dark romance: Moody, dramatic, darker color palette
   - Paranormal: Atmospheric with subtle magical elements
   - Historical: Period-appropriate styling
   - Contemporary: Modern, clean, aspirational
4. **Scene or character description** specific to THIS book
5. **Composition notes:**
   - Title placement (usually top third)
   - Author name placement (bottom)
   - Enough negative space for text readability
   - Vertical/portrait orientation (1600x2560px)

## Formatting

Use the same docx generation approach as the manuscript, but simpler:
- US Letter, 1 inch margins
- Section headings as Heading 1
- Body text in 12pt Times New Roman
- Single spacing is fine for this document
