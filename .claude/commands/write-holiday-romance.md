# Write Holiday Romance Manuscript

This command launches the Holiday Romance Author plugin. It takes your completed holiday romance outline and writes the entire manuscript chapter by chapter, with the Holiday Integration Engine applied throughout.

## What You Need

- **A completed holiday romance outline** (from a holiday romance outline builder or your own outline with all chapters and scene breakdowns)
- **Clarity on:** holiday, subgenre, heat level, relationship type, POV preference, target word count

## What You'll Get

1. **Manuscript (.docx)** — Fully written, edited, formatted, ready for KDP
2. **KDP Metadata (.docx)** — Title, keywords, categories, back-cover copy, publishing timeline, comp titles
3. **Process documentation** — Your Holiday Integration Engine compliance tracking

## The Process

### Step 1: Upload Your Outline
Provide your completed outline. I'll verify:
- All chapters listed with scene breakdowns
- Holiday clearly identified
- Relationship dynamics and heat level noted
- Holiday integration visible in the outline

### Step 2: Answer 8 Setup Questions
1. Holiday (Christmas, Halloween, Valentine's, Thanksgiving, New Year's, etc.)
2. Subgenre (contemporary, small-town, second-chance, workplace, paranormal, etc.)
3. Heat Level (sweet, moderate, steamy, scorching)
4. Relationship Type & POV (MF/FF/MM/Multi, and POV preference)
5. Tense (present or past)
6. Target Word Count (novella, standard, or extended)
7. Holiday Sensory Customization (any specific details for THIS holiday)
8. Specific Requests (tropes to avoid, character professions, scenes to deepen)

### Step 3: Pre-Writing Prep
I prepare:
- Names Avoidance List (replace any overused names)
- Holiday Sensory Palette (specific to your holiday)
- Running Holiday Motif Tracker (recurring holiday details)
- Output folder setup

### Step 4: Write Chapter by Chapter
For each chapter:
- Write full scene breakdown from your outline
- Apply Holiday Integration Engine (all 5 rules)
- Check 10 prose craft rules
- Update trackers
- Move to next chapter

### Step 5: 5-Stage Editorial Pipeline
1. **AI-ism Scrub** — Remove 30 clichéd patterns
2. **Developmental Edit** — Structure assessment (10 dimensions)
3. **Holiday Authenticity Check** — Verify accuracy and cultural sensitivity
4. **Beta Read Simulation** — Reader satisfaction check
5. **Targeted Revision** — Apply top 3 structural fixes

### Step 6: KDP Metadata & Delivery
Generate metadata and deliver both files to your working directory.

## Timeline

- **Novella (20K-50K):** 2-4 hours
- **Standard (70K-90K):** 5-8 hours
- **Extended (90K-130K):** 8-12 hours

## The Holiday Integration Engine (Quick Version)

Every chapter verified against 5 rules:

1. **Holiday as Plot Engine** — Holiday actively shapes what happens
2. **The Holiday Deadline** — Urgency builds toward the holiday moment
3. **Seasonal Emotional Resonance** — Sensory details trigger emotional beats
4. **Holiday-Specific Obstacles** — Problems organic to THIS holiday
5. **The Holiday Resolution** — Climax uses the holiday for maximum impact
